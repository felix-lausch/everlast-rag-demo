Alpine cheese spaetzle pan with pear and bacon baby spinach & cheese chives topping

Courses: Dinner
Categories: Pasta
Collections:
Source: https://www.hellofresh.de/recipes/Alpine-Käsespätzlepfanne-mit-Birne-und-Bacon-650039d5786cce2df0e06f87
Serving size: 2
Preparation time:
Cooking time: 25 mins

Ingredients

400 g fresh egg spaetzle

80 g bacon (died)

1 piece pear

50 g baby spinach

1 piece onion

10 g parsley smooth/chives

50 g spicy Gouda, grated

20 g hard cheese ital. Art, grated

150 g cooking cream

Salt to taste

50 ml of water

Pepper to taste

1 tablespoon oil

Directions

1. Peel the pear, remove the seeds and cut it into 2 cm cubes.

2. Cut the onion in half and cut into fine strips.

3. Finely chop the parsley.

4. Cut chives into fine rolls.

5. Heat a large pan on medium heat without adding fat. Fry bacon strips, onion strips and pear cubes in it for 5 - 7 minutes until the pear is firm to the bite and the onion is soft.

6. Meanwhile, heat 1 tbsp [1.5 tbsp | 2 tbsp] oil* in a second large pan. Spaetzle in it approx. Fry until golden brown for 7 minutes while turning.

7. Deglaze the pear with cooking cream and 50 ml [75 ml | 100 ml] water and approx. Simmer on medium heat for 3 minutes. Season with salt and pepper*.

8. Add Gouda to the sauce and continue to simmer for another 1 minute until the sauce is a little thickened.

9. Gradually add spinach to the sauce in small portions until all the spinach has collapsed.

10. Add fried spaetzle to the sauce, sprinkle chopped parsley over it and turn everything again in the pan. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

11. Spread the spaetzle pan on plates. Sprinkle with grated hard cheese and chives rolls and enjoy.

12. Bon appetit!

GUACAMOLE-SCHAFSKÄSE AUF BACKKARTOFFELN

Courses: Hauptgericht
Categories: Kartoffeln, vegetarisch
Collections:
Source: Kochplan/20
Serving size: 4 Personen
Preparation time: 35 mins
Cooking time:

Ingredients

750 g Kartoffeln

1 EL Olivenöl

Fleisch von 2 reifen Avocados (z. B. Sorte Hass)

½ Zitrone

200 g Schafskäse

2 EL Ajvar (Paprikapaste)

1 TL gemischte TK-Kräuter

200 g Tomaten (wenn keine Tomatenzeit ist, evtl. gekochte Rote Bete klein würfeln oder alternativ Essiggurken verwenden)

Salz, Pfeffer aus der Mühle

Directions

1. Vorbereitung

2. Zu I. Backofen auf 200 °C (Umluft 180 °C) vorheizen.

3. Kartoffeln gut waschen/bürsten und in 1cm dicke Scheiben schneiden.

4. Zu II. Zitrone auspressen

5. Avocados öffnen und Fruchtfleisch herauslöffeln.

6. Zu III. Tomaten halbieren, Stielansatz und Fruchtfleisch entnehmen. Die Tomaten klein würfeln.

7. Kochvorgang

8. I. Ein Blech mit Backpapier auslegen.

9. Kartoffeln mit den Schnittflächen darauf legen. Mit Olivenöl bestreichen und mit Meersalz sowie mit Pfeffer würzen. Im Backofen in knapp 25 Minuten garen.

10. II. Avocadofruchtfleisch mit Zitronensaft sowie mit Schafskäse und Ajvar mit dem Pürierstab zerkleinern. Kräuter unterziehen und mit Meersalz und Pfeffer würzen.

11. III. Die gebackenen Kartoffelscheiben auf einer Platte anrichten.

12. Den Guacamole-Schafskäse und die Tomatenwürfel jeweils in eine Schale füllen und alles zusammen servieren.

Notes

RESTETIPP:

In diesem Rezept können Sie auch angebrochene Becher saurer Sahne aufbrauchen (von Seite 14) anstatt mit Schafskäse die Avocadocreme mit 100 g saurer Sahne zubereiten.

VARIATIONEN

Kartoffeln jeweils mit

- Gepresstem Knoblauch

- Geräuchertem Paprika

- Parmesan

- Pul Biber

vor dem backen toppen.

Halloumi and Mediterranean veg traybake

Courses: Dinner
Categories: Vegetable, Vegetarian
Collections:
Source: https://www.olivemagazine.com/recipes/vegetarian/halloumi-and-mediterranean-veg-traybake/?dicbo=v2-DPWNxxS
Serving size: Serves 2
Preparation time: 20 mins
Cooking time: 25 mins

Ingredients

2 courgettes, cut into small chunks

1/2 red onion, cut into slim wedges

1 1/2 large cloves garlic, skins left on

1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil

150g cherry tomatoes

125g halloumi, sliced

0.13 teaspoon cumin seeds

a few pinches dried chilli flakes (optional)

flatbreads, to serve

hummus and hot sauce, to serve

Directions

1. Heat the oven to 200C/fan 180C/gas 6. Toss the courgettes, onion and garlic with the olive oil, and tip into a large, shallow baking dish. Season well and roast for 10 minutes.

2. Stir in the cherry tomatoes and sit the halloumi slices on top. Sprinkle over the cumin seeds and chilli flakes, if using. Cook for another 15 minutes until the tomatoes start to burst and release juice, and the halloumi is turning golden.

3. Squeeze the garlic cloves out of their skins and mash into the oil and juices in the baking dish, stirring it in. Spoon everything onto plates and serve with soft flatbreads and hummus or hot sauce, if you like.

Classic Buttery Mashed Potato

Courses: Dinner, Side Dish
Categories: Side Dish
Collections:
Source: https://myfoodbook.com.au/recipes/show/classic-buttery-mashed-potato
Serving size: 4
Preparation time: 10 mins
Cooking time: 25 mins

Ingredients

1kg potatoes

100g Western Star Original Salted butter diced

½ cup (125ml) warm milk

Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

1. Wash and peel potatoes and cut into large chunks. Place in a medium saucepan and just cover with cold water. Bring to the boil then simmer for 20-25 minutes or until fork tender

2. Drain potatoes and return to the dry pan. Shake pan occasionally to remove excess moisture. Mash the potatoes and stir in ¾ of the butter

3. Gradually add milk and beat with a wooden spoon until smooth. Season with salt and pepper. Serve topped with remaining butter
